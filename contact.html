What's your typical response time?

I usually respond to inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent projects, feel free to mention it in your message.

Yes! I'm available for travel within India and internationally for the right projects. Travel costs would be discussed separately.

Each package is customized based on your needs, but typically includes planning, shooting, basic editing, and digital delivery. We can discuss specifics based on your project.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, for larger projects I offer flexible payment plans. We can discuss terms that work for both of us.

Can you help with content strategy?

Absolutely! I can help develop content strategies for social media, blogs, and other platforms based on your goals and audience.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

No worries! I offer consultation calls where we can discuss your goals and I can recommend the best approach for your project.
