Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's your typical response time?

I usually respond to inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent projects, feel free to mention it in your message and I'll prioritize getting back to you faster.

Do you travel for projects?

Yes! I'm available for travel within India and internationally for the right projects. Travel costs would be discussed separately based on location and project requirements.

What's included in your photography packages?

Each package is customized based on your needs, but typically includes pre-shoot planning, on-site shooting, professional editing, and digital delivery. We can discuss specifics based on your project scope.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, for larger projects I offer flexible payment plans. Typically a 30-50% upfront deposit with the remaining balance split into milestones. We can discuss terms that work for both of us.

Can you help with content strategy?

Absolutely! I can help develop comprehensive content strategies for social media, blogs, and other platforms based on your goals, target audience, and brand identity.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

No worries! I offer free consultation calls where we can discuss your goals, challenges, and vision. I'll recommend the best approach and services for your specific needs.
