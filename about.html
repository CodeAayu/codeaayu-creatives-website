My Creative Journey

From engineering to visual storytelling

My journey into photography and creative content began during my college years at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, where I pursued an Integrated MTech in Mathematics and Computing. What started as a hobby quickly became a passion that would shape my creative identity.

As a travel and portrait photographer, I'm drawn to capturing authentic moments and telling stories through my lens. Whether it's the candid expression of a stranger on the street, the golden hour light painting a mountain landscape, or the intimate moment during a portrait session, I strive to create images that resonate with viewers and evoke emotion.

My background in technology and current role in software development at udaan.com has given me a unique perspective on the intersection of creativity and technology. This dual experience helps me approach visual storytelling with both artistic intuition and technical precision.

Beyond photography, I'm passionate about writing and content creation. Through my articles on Medium, I share insights about creativity, personal growth, travel experiences, and technology. I believe in building a community of fellow creatives and learning from each other's journeys.
