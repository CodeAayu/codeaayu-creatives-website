About Me

Turning moments into timeless visual stories

Hey! I'm Aayush, a passionate photographer and creative content creator with a love for capturing authentic moments and telling compelling stories through my lens.

My journey started during my time at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, where I pursued Integrated MTech in Mathematics and Computing. What began as a hobby evolved into a full-fledged passion for visual storytelling.

Currently working as a Software Engineer at udaan.com, I blend my technical expertise with artistic vision to create images that resonate. Whether it's golden hour landscapes, intimate portraits, or candid street moments, I strive to capture emotion and authenticity.
